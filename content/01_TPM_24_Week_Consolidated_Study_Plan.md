# 📅 TPM 24-Week Consolidated Study Plan

> **Purpose:** A week-by-week, day-by-day plan that tells you exactly what to study, in what order, using only the files in this directory. No internet required.
>
> **Daily time:** 2 hours (adjustable - complete the week's work regardless)
> **Weekly time:** ~14 hours
> **Total program:** ~336 hours across 24 weeks
>
> **How to use this plan:** Each week has daily assignments. Check off items as you complete them. Revisit any week you find difficult before moving on.

---

## How the 24 Weeks Are Organized

| Phase | Weeks | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **Phase 1: Foundation** | 1–6 | Core frameworks, vocabulary, and TPM mindset |
| **Phase 2: Deep Dive** | 7–12 | Each TPM competency area explored in depth |
| **Phase 3: Application** | 13–18 | Case studies, practical application, portfolio building |
| **Phase 4: Interview Prep** | 19–24 | Stories, mock interviews, final preparation |
